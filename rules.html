Game Rules

Welcome to the Memory Card Game! The goal of this game is to match all of the pairs of cards by taking minimum number of guesses. Here are the rules:

How to Play

To play the game, simply click on a card to flip it over and reveal the image on the other side. Then, click on another card to reveal its image. If the two images match, the cards will stay face up and you can make another guess. If the two images do not match, the cards will flip back over and you will need to try again.

Gameplay Tips

  • Pay attention to the images on each card and remember their locations in order to match them more quickly.

  • Try to memorize the locations of the matching pairs so that you can flip them over quickly when you see them.

  • Don't be afraid to take your time! The game is not timed, so you can take as long as you need to match all of the cards.

Invalid Moves

  • Revealing more than two cards in single go leads to invalid move.

  • Tapping on an already opened card leads to invalid move.

Winning the Game

You win the game by matching all of the pairs of cards . Good luck!
